CLIENT ONBOARDING POLICY (MOCK)

Scope
This policy defines the standard steps for onboarding a new institutional
client into the wealth and markets business.

1. Client Intake
   - Capture legal name, entity type, country of incorporation and
     regulatory identifiers.
   - Confirm the requested products and expected trading limits.

2. KYC / AML Screening
   - Verify beneficial ownership for entities above the 25% threshold.
   - Screen the client and related parties against sanctions and PEP lists.
   - Assign a risk rating of Low, Medium or High based on jurisdiction,
     product complexity and ownership structure.

3. Entity Resolution
   - Match the client against existing records to avoid duplicate entities.
   - Link parent, subsidiary and affiliate relationships.

4. Financial Review
   - Validate credit rating and requested trading limit against the
     approved credit policy.
   - Escalate any limit request above the delegated authority threshold.

5. Approval
   - A case is marked ReadyToTrade only after intake, KYC/AML, entity
     resolution and financial review are all complete.
