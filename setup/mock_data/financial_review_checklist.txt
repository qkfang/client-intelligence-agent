FINANCIAL REVIEW AND CREDIT LIMIT CHECKLIST (MOCK)

Purpose
Defines the checks performed before a requested trading limit is
approved for a new or existing client.

Checklist
1. Confirm the client's credit rating is current (refreshed within the
   last 12 months).
2. Compare the requested trading limit against the credit policy matrix
   for the client's credit rating and product mix.
3. Confirm collateral or margin arrangements are in place for products
   that require them.
4. Escalate to the credit committee when the requested limit exceeds the
   delegated approval authority for the reviewing officer.
5. Record the approved limit, approving officer and review date on the
   client record.

Credit Policy Matrix (indicative)
- Rating A: up to 50,000,000 in unsecured trading limit.
- Rating B: up to 20,000,000 in unsecured trading limit.
- Rating C: unsecured limits require credit committee approval; secured
  limits only above this rating.
